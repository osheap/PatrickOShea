Paradise Valley Trail Run Map

This is a trail map for the annual Paradise Valley Trail Run, south of San Francisco. The map is geared towards trail runners and gives information about the 5K and 10K routes, locations of the start/finish line and water stations, and an elevation profile. Colors and cartographic styling choices balance providing runners with valuable information while also conveying the sense fun an excitement this event is known for.

Black Bayou Lake National Wildlife Refuge Trail Map.

This is a trail map designed for hikers along the Edgewater Trail, located within the Black Bayou Lake National Wildlife Refuge. The map displays the trail prominently within the main map while identifying surrounding natural and man-made landmarks. The inset map in the bottom left locates the Edgewater Trail relative to the greater Monroe area in Northern Louisiana.

Health Insurance Coverage Rates Amongst Whites and Latinos in New England

This map shows the rates of insurance coverage for Hispanics, and non-hispanic whites relative to the national average of 92% at the county level. The results show that non-hispanic whites are either at or above the average rate, while hispanics have significantly more varied coverage rates.
